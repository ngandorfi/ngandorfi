Our training programs


Start Puppy: Social and friendly dogs

Socialisation is a very important aspect of a dog’s life. Learning how to respond to other dogs, and what is acceptable and not acceptable in dog language is an essential life lesson they need to understand and know if they are to get along with other dogs.

Intermediate: Easy Management

Obedience schools teach basic commands (e.g. sit, drop, stay, shake, etc.). This commands will allow you to manage your dog more easily while you are out on a walk or at home, they'll build a stronger owner/pet bond and also will pop up your dog's confidence!

Advanced: Owner socialisation and community growth

Going to obedience training every week gets you out and about meeting people from your neighbourhood and community. Statistics show that people who have dogs are at a lower risk of physical and psychological health problems including lowered risk of cardiovascular disease, hypertension, loneliness and anxiety.

Master: Competition

We love all dogs but the fact is some breeds are naturally better at performing certain functions than others. Some have innate skills as hunting companions; others assist in the herding of livestock; and others serve as guardians of people and property. In this course, we will improve the skills to your dog.
